Find two distinct square roots of $i$.
# Solution
$$
i &= (a + b i)(a + b i)\
&= a^2 - b^2 + 2 a b i
$$
Requiring that $a^2 - b^2 = 0$ and $2 a b = 1$. Consider
$$
(1 + i)^2
&= (1 + i)(1 + i)\
&= (1 - 1) + 2i = 2i
$$
, then$$(plus.minus (1 + i) / sqrt(2))^2 = i$$
