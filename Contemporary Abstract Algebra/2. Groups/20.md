List the members of $K = \{x^2 : x \in D_4\}$ and $L = \{x \in D_4 : x^2 = e\}$.
##### Solution:
- $K = \{x^2 : x \in D_4\}$:
	$K=\{R_0,R_{180}\}$
- $L = \{x \in D_4 : x^2 = e\}$:
	$L = \{R_0,R_{180},F_V,F_H,F_D,F_{D'}\}$
