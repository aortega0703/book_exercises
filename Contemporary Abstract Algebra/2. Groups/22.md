For any integer $n > 2$, show that there are at least two elements in $U(n)$ that satisfy $x^2 = 1$.
##### Solution:
- $1\in U(n)$ and $1^2\equiv 1\ \operatorname{mod}\ n$.
- $n-1\in U(n)$ and $(n-1)^2\equiv n^2 - 2n + 1 \equiv 1\ \operatorname{mod}\ n$.
