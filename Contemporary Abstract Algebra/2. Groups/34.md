Give an example of a group with exactly $105$ elements. Give three examples of groups with $40$ elements.
##### Solution:
- $|G|=105$:
	$| \mathbb{Z}/105| = 105$.
- $|G|=40$:
	- $|\mathbb{Z}/40|=40$.
	- $|D_{2(20)}|=40$ by [[Dihedral group order]].
	- $|U(41)|=40$ as $41$ is prime.
