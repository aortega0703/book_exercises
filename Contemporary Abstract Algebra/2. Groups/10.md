List the elements of $U(20)$ and find the inverse of each one.
##### Solution:
$$
U(20) = \{1,3,7,9,11,13,17,19\}
$$
- $1^{-1}= 1$
- $3^{-1}=7$
- $7^{-1}=3$
- $9^{-1}=9$
- $11^{-1}=11$
- $13^{-1}=17$
- $17^{-1}=13$
- $19^{-1}=19$
