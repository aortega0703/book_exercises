Suppose that $a$ and $b$ belong to a group and $a^5 = e$ and $b^7 = e$. Write $a^{−2} b^{−4}$  and $(a^2 b^4 )^{−2}$ without using negative exponents.
##### Solution:
- $a^{−2} b^{−4}$:$$
	\begin{align}
		a^{−2} b^{−4} 
			&= a^{−2} e b^{−4} e\\
			&= a^{−2} a^5 b^{−4} b^7\\
			&= a^3 b^3
	\end{align}
	$$
- $(a^2 b^4 )^{−2}$:$$
	\begin{align}
	(a^2 b^4 )^{−2}
	&= ((a^2 b^4 )^{−1})^2\\
	&= (b^{-4} a^{-2})^2\\
	&= (b^3 a^3)^2\\
	\end{align}
	$$
