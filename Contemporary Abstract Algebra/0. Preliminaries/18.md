Determine $7^{1000}\ op("mod") 6$ and $6^{1001}\ op("mod")7$.
##### Solution:
- $7^{1000}\ op("mod") 6 = 1$:
	$7\equiv 1\ (op("mod")6)$, so $7^{1000}\equiv 1^{1000}\equiv 1\ (op("mod") 6)$.
- $6^{1001} \ op("mod") 7 = 6$:
	$6 \equiv -1\ op("mod 7")$, so $6^{1001} \equiv -1^{1001} \equiv -1 \equiv 6\ op("mod") 7$.
