Suppose that $m$ and $n$ are relatively prime and $r$ is any integer. Show that there are integers $x$ and $y$ such that $mx+ ny = r$.
##### Solution:
By [[Relative Primes Iff Linear Combination Equals 1]] for some $s,t in ZZ$$$

	ms + nt &= 1\
	m(sr) + n(tr) &= r

$$So $x=sr$ and $y=tr$.
