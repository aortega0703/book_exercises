In $D_4$, find all elements $X$ such that 
1. $X^3 = V$; 
2. $X^3 = R_{90}$;
3. $X^3 = R_0$;
4. $X^2 = R_0$;
5. $X^2 = H$.
##### Solution:
1. $V$.
2. $R_{270}$.
3. $R_0$.
4. $R_0$, $R_{180}$, $H$, $V$, $D$, $D'$.
5. None.
