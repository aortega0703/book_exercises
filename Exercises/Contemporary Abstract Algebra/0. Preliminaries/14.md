Show that $5n+3$ and $7n + 4$ are relatively prime for all $n$.
##### Solution:
$(5n + 3)7 + (7n + 4)(-5)= (35n + 21) - (35n + 20)= 1$ so by [[Relative Primes Iff Linear Combination Equals 1]] they are relatively prime for all $n\in\mathbb{Z}$.
