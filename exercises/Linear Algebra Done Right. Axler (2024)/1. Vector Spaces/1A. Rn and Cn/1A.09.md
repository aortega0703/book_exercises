Find $x in RR^4$ such that$$
(4, -3, 1, 7) + 2x = (5, 9, -6, 8)
$$
# Solution
$$
(4, -3, 1, 7) + 2x &= (5, 9, -6, 8)\
2x &= (5, 9, -6, 8) - (4, -3, 1, 7)\
x &= ((1, 12, -7, 1)) / 2\
x &= (1 / 2, 6, (-7)/2, 1 /2)
$$
