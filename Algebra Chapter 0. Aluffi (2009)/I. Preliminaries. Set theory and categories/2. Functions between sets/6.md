With notation as in Example 2.4, explain how any function $f : A â†’ B$ determines a section of $\pi_A$.
##### Solution:
Let$$

	g:A &\to A\times B\
	a &\mapsto (a, f(a))

$$then$$

	(\pi_A\circ g)(a)
	&= \pi_A(a, f(a))\
	&= a\
	\pi_A\circ g &= \operatorname{id}_A

$$so $g$ is a section of $\pi_A$.
